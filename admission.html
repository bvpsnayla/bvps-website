Mandatory

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, call us directly.

The school fees vary by class level - Primary (I-V), Middle (VI-VIII), Secondary (IX-X), and Senior Secondary (XI-XII). Fees include tuition, activity, and development charges. Please contact our office at +91 9783318862 or visit in person for the complete fee structure. We also have provisions for fee concessions for meritorious and economically weaker students.

Yes, BVPS provides safe and reliable school transport services covering Nayla and nearby villages and areas in Jaipur district. Transport fees depend on the distance from the school. Please contact the school office for bus route details and applicable charges.

School timings are Monday to Saturday, 7:30 AM - 2:00 PM (subject to seasonal adjustments). The school office is open from 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM. School remains closed on Sundays and all government holidays declared by the Government of Rajasthan and RBSE.

For Classes XI and XII, BVPS offers three streams: Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics), Arts (Hindi, English, History, Geography, Political Science, Economics), and Commerce (Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics). Stream selection is made at the time of Class XI admission.

For the 2025-26 academic session, the last date for submitting the admission form is 31st March 2025. However, admissions are on a first-come, first-served basis and seats are limited. We strongly recommend applying as early as possible.

Absolutely! BVPS offers a wide range of co-curricular activities including Sports (Cricket, Kabaddi, Athletics), Cultural Activities (Drama, Dance, Music), Academic Clubs (Science, Maths, Literary), and Personality Development Programs. Annual Sports Day, Cultural Fest, and Science Exhibitions are hallmark events at BVPS.
