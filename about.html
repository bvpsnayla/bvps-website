Our Story

About Our School

Bal Vikas Senior Secondary School (BVPS) is a co-educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering the holistic development of students. Established in 2000, the school has been committed to nurturing young minds through academic excellence, discipline, and strong moral values.

The school believes that education is not limited to textbooks but extends to character building, creativity, leadership, and social responsibility. BVPS provides a supportive learning environment where students are encouraged to explore their talents and develop confidence.

With experienced teachers, structured academic programs, and a balanced approach to co-curricular activities, the school aims to prepare students for both academic success and life beyond the classroom.

Leadership Message

Founder / Director's Message

Founder & Director

BVPS, Nayla

"Education is the foundation upon which the future of every individual and society is built. At Bal Vikas Senior Secondary School, our aim is not only to provide academic knowledge but also to nurture character, discipline, and confidence in every student."

Since the establishment of our institution in 2000, we have remained committed to creating an environment where students can grow intellectually, morally, and socially. We believe that true education goes beyond textbooks and examinations — it is about developing curiosity, responsibility, and the ability to think independently.

Our dedicated teachers, supportive learning environment, and balanced approach to academics and co-curricular activities help students discover their talents and reach their full potential. Guided by our motto "A Great Place to Learn," we remain devoted to shaping responsible citizens and future leaders.
