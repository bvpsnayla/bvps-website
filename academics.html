Our Academic Program

The academic program at BVPS focuses on developing strong foundational knowledge, critical thinking, and lifelong learning skills aligned with RBSE guidelines while ensuring holistic development.

Overview

Academic Overview

Bal Vikas Senior Secondary School provides a well-structured academic program from Class I to Class XII, affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE). Our curriculum is designed to foster conceptual understanding, practical application, and character development. We believe education extends beyond textbooks — it's about building confident, disciplined, and responsible citizens who are ready for the challenges of tomorrow. With experienced faculty, modern teaching methodologies, and a supportive learning environment, we ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Assessment

Evaluation System

Our comprehensive evaluation system goes beyond traditional exams. We assess students through continuous, multi-dimensional methods to get a complete picture of their academic progress and overall development.

1
Unit Tests

Regular chapter-wise assessments conducted throughout the term to monitor continuous learning progress.

2
Half Yearly Examination

Comprehensive mid-session examination covering the entire first-half syllabus with detailed performance analysis.

3
Annual Examination

Final examination covering the complete syllabus, preparing students for board-level assessment patterns.

4
Continuous Assessment

Ongoing evaluation through class participation, homework, projects, assignments, and practical work.
